About the Group

NCT is a South Korean boy group formed by S.M. Entertainment. Their name stands for Neo Culture Technology, a term coined by S.M. Entertainment founder Lee Soo-man to describe the group's concept of having an unlimited number of members divided into multiple sub-units based in various cities worldwide (sourced from Wikipedia). They are divided into four units so far: NCT U, NCT 127, NCT Dream, and NCT 2018.
